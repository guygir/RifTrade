- **Weekly Actions**: 3 actions per week (training, buying/selling players, tactics, skills, infrastructure)
- **Game Simulation**: Play vs AI teams each week
- **Aging System**: Players age 18‚Üí21, retire at 21, draft new 18-year-olds
